ABOUT ME

My official name is Felix Tertius Teepe, which means "the lucky third" in Latin, I am the third child of my parents so that explains my name. Most people know me as Terts, which is my nickname. I was born at 24th of july in 1991 and I have been living in Amsterdam since then.

After I finished finshed my highschool I took a gab year. Within this year I travelled together with my friend Mark for 5 months. We started in Hawaii, from there we went on to New-Zealand, China, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia and Indonesia. Last year, after I finished my Bachelor Degree in 'Beta-Gamma - Neuroschience' at the University of Amsterdam, I went to South-America for about 5 months. During my travels I discovered that I like the art of photography a lot.

Luckily I have seen many beautiful places and met a lot of wonderful people during my travels. Fascinated by the landscapes I've seen in Azia, Africa and South-America I started taking pictures. For now I am mainly focussing on landscape and nature photos. Recently I started taking potrait photos aswell.
